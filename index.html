My Interests

Hello! My name is Maryia. I have several hobbies that bring me joy:

  • Drawing – I like sketching and expressing myself through art.
  • Reading – I enjoy books that teach and inspire.
  • Doing sports – Staying active helps me feel good and healthy.

I also love learning new languages and creating beautiful things using HTML and CSS.
This is my third project, but I haven't worked with HTML and CSS in a long time.
It's really nice to get back to it — I actually enjoy working with this kind of stuff.

Drawing is one of my biggest passions. I enjoy sketching, illustrating, and experimenting with different techniques like pencil, watercolor, and digital art. It helps me express emotions and ideas in a creative way.

I love reading both fiction and educational books. One of my favorite authors is Richard Feynman, a brilliant physicist known for his inspiring way of explaining complex ideas.

I enjoy staying active. Every day I go to a fitness studio for pilates and stretching. I also love cycling and walking on sunny days — it keeps me relaxed and energized.
